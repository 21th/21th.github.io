Frequently Asked Questions
How does the AI in PeakPicks work to find the best products on Amazon?
Our AI leverages big data analytics and machine learning algorithms to analyze various factors such as current market trends, historical sales data, price changes, and customer reviews. It continuously scans the Amazon marketplace and external sources to identify products that offer the maximum profit potential based on the chosen strategy (high-risk, medium-risk, low-risk).
What do you mean by high-risk, medium-risk, and low-risk strategies?
These strategies represent different approaches to product selection based on your risk tolerance:

High-risk strategy: Targets products with higher potential returns but involves a greater risk of fluctuations and competition.

Medium-risk strategy: Offers a balanced approach, focusing on products with a moderate level of risk and return.

Low-risk strategy: Focuses on products with stable demand and lower competition, offering steady, albeit potentially smaller, profits.
Can I switch between different risk strategies?
Absolutely, you can switch between different risk strategies at any time with Standart subscriptions to align with your current business objectives and market conditions.
